# modbus

附件PK开头，改后缀.zip，解压出一个没有后缀的文件，file识别为流量包，用wireshark打开

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/39170111/1763538194484-5ad6f335-6cc9-40ae-a1f3-7536bab2d412.png)![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/39170111/1763538250742-b14d73aa-6334-4b71-97aa-c597dd61b001.png)

modbus.func\_code == 16筛出来所有Write Multiple Registers

猜测寄存器value为温度

再筛选出寄存器中温度为620的，modbus.func\_code == 16 && modbus.regval\_float==620.00

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/39170111/1763541014442-d2bbde4c-4ed9-40ce-89f5-dd9dfa4882c3.png)

再去查找对应寄存器Register 22的其他记录，modbus.func\_code == 16 && modbus.regnum32==22

找到另一个数据包：Register 22 (IEEE Float): 639.559998大于620

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/39170111/1763541099788-40078da2-3fe3-41c1-ae3b-1b1333a35d6c.png)

复制对应Modbus/TCP hex stream，得到flag

flag{0c010000000b01100016000204441fe3d7}
